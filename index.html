About

Creative tech
meets strategy

I'm a Creative Strategist based in Stockholm, currently working at Adsome Film — a digital production studio replacing traditional ad production with AI-driven workflows.

My work spans the full pipeline: brand analysis, creative brief, prompt engineering, image and video generation, layout, and client delivery. I've produced assets for 30+ brands across beauty, lifestyle, food & beverage, and jewellery.

Background in enterprise IT and music production — I bring systems thinking and a trained creative eye to everything I build.
